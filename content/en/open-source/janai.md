## Introduction

As AI becomes an integral part of our daily lives, two main obstacles remain for users: **privacy** and **subscription costs**. Every word you type on cloud-based chatbots is stored on company servers. But what if you could have the full power of ChatGPT on your personal computer, offline, with 100% privacy?

This is where **Jan** comes in, a project that revolutionized local AI and gained widespread adoption among developers and creators.

## What is Jan?

**Jan** is an open-source platform designed to run large language models (LLMs) directly on your computer. Instead of sending your data to an external server, Jan turns your machine into a personal AI server.

The project has built strong trust, with over **5.2 million downloads** and more than **40,000 stars on GitHub**, making it the top choice for anyone seeking a "personal AI."

## How does the tool work?

Jan provides an easy-to-use graphical interface that loads and runs models using your computer’s resources (CPU or GPU).

The tool uses technologies like `llama.cpp` to ensure efficient performance even on mid-range devices. Once a model (like Llama 3 or DeepSeek) is downloaded through the app, you can disconnect from the Internet completely and continue chatting, programming, or analyzing text as if you were connected to the most powerful servers.

## Key Features of Jan

* **Absolute Privacy:** your data never leaves your device. All chats and files are processed locally.
* **Completely Free:** no API bills or paid subscriptions. You only pay for your computer’s electricity consumption.
* **Support for Global Models:** choose from powerful models like **Llama, Qwen, Gemma,** and **DeepSeek** and switch them with a single click.
* **Web Search Integration:** while running locally, Jan can fetch up-to-date information from the web when needed.
* **Integration with Work Tools:** connect Jan with your favorite apps like **Notion, Gmail, Google Drive** to manage tasks smartly.

## Who benefits from this tool?

1. **Developers:** get coding assistance without leaking sensitive scripts.
2. **Writers & Researchers:** process ideas and drafts with complete privacy.
3. **Students:** access powerful AI even in areas with poor Internet connectivity.
4. **Companies:** create an internal smart assistant for employees without relying on external services.

## Practical Example: How to Start

After installing Jan, go to the "Model Hub", choose the **DeepSeek-R1** model (renowned for its high efficiency) and click "Download". Once downloaded, open a new chat and ask: *"Write a business plan for a tech project."* You’ll notice a very fast response, and most importantly, you can do this in “Airplane Mode.”

## Conclusion

**Jan** demonstrates that the future of AI is not limited to big corporations. It can be accessible to everyone, everywhere. Combining ease of use, powerful open-source models, and strict privacy, having a local AI becomes a necessity rather than just a technical option.
