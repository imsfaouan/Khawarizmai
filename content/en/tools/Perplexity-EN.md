## What is Perplexity AI?

Perplexity AI is an **Answer Engine**, not a traditional search engine.

The fundamental difference is simple:

- **Google** gives you a list of links — you read and compile the information yourself.
- **Perplexity** reads those links for you and gives you the ready-made answer — with a source for every sentence.

Perplexity reached **one billion queries per month** in the first quarter of 2026. This is not random growth — it’s proof that millions of people found something in it that they couldn’t find in Google.

## Why Perplexity Is Different — 5 Core Features

## 1. Transparency and Sources — The Strongest Feature

Every sentence Perplexity writes has a **number next to it** that links you to the original source.

Why is this extremely important?  
It solves the “hallucination” problem that other AI models suffer from. When ChatGPT makes a mistake, you don’t know — when Perplexity makes a mistake, you discover it immediately because every piece of information is documented.

## 2. Real-Time Web Search

Unlike the free versions of ChatGPT and Claude that work from outdated data, **Perplexity searches the web in real time** and answers you about news that happened minutes ago.

This difference creates a completely different world when searching for:
- Stock and currency prices
- Latest tech news
- Match results
- Any information that changes quickly

## 3. Choose the Model That Thinks for You

In the Pro version, you can choose the “brain” that answers you:
- **GPT-4o** — for creative tasks
- **Claude 3.5** — for writing and analysis
- **Gemini** — for multimodal tasks
- **Perplexity’s own models** — for fast research

## 4. Copilot — It Asks You Instead of You Guessing

If your question is vague, Perplexity doesn’t guess — it **asks you clarifying questions** first.

Example: If you write “best phone”, it will ask you:
- What is your budget?
- What is the most important feature you’re looking for?
- In which country will you buy it?

Result? An answer tailored specifically to you.

## 5. Pages — Turn Your Research Into an Organized Article

A unique feature that lets you turn search results into an **organized “Page”** with titles, paragraphs, and images — which can be shared with others as a complete article.

Very useful for preparing reports, summarizing research, or creating content based on real data.

## Perplexity vs Google

The real difference between the two is not in the technology — but in the **philosophy of answering**.

- **Google** assumes you want to read and decide for yourself. It gives you ten sources and leaves you.
- **Perplexity** assumes you want the answer directly. It reads the sources for you and crafts an answer.

The only problem with Perplexity?  
If you’re looking for a specific commercial site or want to shop or search for images… Google is still better.  
But for every real question that needs a fast and reliable answer — **Perplexity wins**.
