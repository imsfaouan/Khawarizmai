## Why FreeCut is Different from Traditional Cloud Tools

Most "Online Editors" force you to upload your files to their servers. That means long wait times and privacy risks. **FreeCut** flips the script:

* **Local Processing:** Your files never leave your device. The browser talks directly to your hardware.
* **Desktop-Grade Speed:** Powered by **WebGPU** and **WebCodecs**, the editing experience is as smooth as native desktop apps.
* **Privacy First:** Since the export happens locally, your data is 100% secure.
