## Step 1: Writing a "Powerful" Script with Claude AI

The script is the backbone. If the script is boring, even the best graphics in the world will not save you. In 2026, we move away from traditional stories and focus on "shocking realism".

Using smart prompts

Instead of saying "write me a horror story", use the "layer technique". Ask Claude to act as an award-winning mystery screenplay writer.

Tip from **Khawarizmai experience:** always start your story with an event that happens in the very first second. Example: "The moment I opened the door, I found a message written in my blood saying: don't look behind you."

Use open source AI models like Llama 3 available on Hugging Face if you want a private and unrestricted environment. They are among the best Hugging Face alternatives when it comes to customizing local stories (like the legends I used to write about).

## Step 2: Designing Characters Smartly (Nano Banana 2)

A common mistake beginners make is using generic images. To succeed in "Beginner AI projects", your character must be unique.

Use the Nano Banana 2 model (also known as Gemini 3 Flash Image) to create professional 3D characters in a "Blender-style" look.

Professional character prompt:

3D character design, professional lighting, cinematic horror atmosphere, expressive eyes, realistic textures, 8k resolution, vertical 9:16 --ar 9:16

---

## Step 3: Turning Still Images into Motion (Veo & Kling)

This is where the magic happens. Turning an image into a video (Image-to-Video) is what separates professionals from amateurs.

**Veo:** Google's newest tool that provides high-quality cinematic movement. (Available for Ultra and Pro subscribers).

**Kling AI:** If you want complex movements like "eating" or "running", it is currently the best option.

When using these tools, focus on "small facial movements". A blink, a mysterious smile, or a slight head turn is enough to create tension.

---

## Step 4: The Voice That Creates Chills (ElevenLabs & Lyria 3)

Voice represents 50% of the video experience. Never use old robotic voices.

In ElevenLabs, search for voices like "Marcus" or "Bella" and adjust "Stability" and "Exaggeration" to get a dramatic tone.

For background music, in 2026 we rely on Lyria 3 to generate unique 30-second tracks that change with the pace of the story.

---

## Step 5: Editing and Virality (The Virality Formula)

Use CapCut to combine everything. The secret in 2026 is **Auto Captions**. Words must appear clearly in the middle of the screen with motion effects.

Publishing strategy:

**TikTok:** post 3 times daily during peak hours (7 PM to 11 PM).

**YouTube Shorts:** focus on SEO in the title.

**Hashtags:** use a mix of #AI_Horror #Mystery #Storytelling.

## FAQs: Frequently Asked Questions

**1. Can I start completely for free?**  
Yes, you can use Claude for scripting, Leonardo AI for images, and Meta AI for basic animation. However, professional quality will require a small investment later.

**2. Does TikTok reject AI-generated content?**  
On the contrary, TikTok supports creativity, but you should label "AI Generated Content" if the video looks very realistic to avoid account restrictions.

**3. What is the best niche right now?**  
"True Crime" stories and "mysterious disappearance" stories are the most popular in 2026.

**4. How can I get endless ideas?**  
Ask Claude to analyze "Reddit NoSleep" and extract trending ideas, then rewrite them in your own style.
