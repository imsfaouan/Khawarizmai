## What is Sniffnet?

Sniffnet is an open-source tool designed for **monitoring internet traffic** in a comfortable and simple way.

Instead of using complex tools like Wireshark, Sniffnet gives you a modern and intuitive interface that shows you:
- All active connections right now
- Which app or device is consuming internet
- The protocols and services being used
- The amount of data sent and received
- The websites and servers your devices are communicating with
- The **geographical location** of remote hosts (a very powerful feature)

All of this helps you detect apps that are “spying” or sending your data without your knowledge, and know who is stealing your internet.

## Installation Method (Very Easy)

Sniffnet is one of the easiest tools to install, just like any regular program:

### On Windows:
- Download the file `Sniffnet_Windows_x64.msi`
- Run it and follow the simple steps

### On macOS:
- Download the appropriate `.dmg` file (Intel or Apple Silicon)
- Drag it to the Applications folder

### On Linux:
