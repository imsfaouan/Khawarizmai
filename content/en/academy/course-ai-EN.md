## Introduction

**Artificial Intelligence (AI)** has become an essential part of our daily lives, from search engines and online recommendations to smart assistants and modern applications. With this rapid growth, understanding the basics of artificial intelligence has become an important skill, even for people without programming knowledge.

The good news is that many universities and major technology companies now offer **free AI courses for beginners**, without requiring any programming experience. In this article, we will explore three of the best courses that help you understand this field in a simple and practical way.

---

## What are beginner AI courses?

Beginner AI courses are educational programs designed to explain the fundamental concepts of artificial intelligence using simple language, without going deep into complex programming details. The great advantage is that they are accessible to learners worldwide.

These courses usually focus on:

- Understanding how artificial intelligence works  
- Learning about its applications in everyday life  
- Studying the basics of **Machine Learning**  
- Understanding the impact of AI on business and society  

These courses are suitable for students, entrepreneurs, marketers, or anyone who wants to understand or enter this field and learn about the technology that is transforming the world.

## Best Artificial Intelligence Courses for Beginners

### 1. AI for Everyone

![Coursera Academy](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/imsfaouan/images-kh/refs/heads/main/coursera%20academy.png)

The **AI for Everyone** course is considered one of the best introductory courses for understanding artificial intelligence. It was developed by the **DeepLearning.AI** platform and presented by a well-known expert in the field.

The goal of the course is to explain the core concepts of artificial intelligence in a simple way while focusing on how companies can use this technology.

During the course, you will learn:

- What artificial intelligence is and how it works  
- The difference between AI and machine learning  
- How companies can build an AI strategy  
- Ethical challenges related to artificial intelligence  

You can study the course content for free using the **Audit** option on Coursera, and you can also apply for **Financial Aid** to obtain the certificate for free.

🔗 **Course link:** [Click here to visit the website](https://www.coursera.org)

---

### 2. Elements of AI

![Coursera Academy](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/imsfaouan/images-kh/refs/heads/main/building%20academy.png)

The **Elements of AI** course is one of the most popular free courses in the world for learning the fundamentals of artificial intelligence.

It was developed through a collaboration between the **University of Helsinki** and the company **Reaktor**, with support from several global technology organizations.

What makes this course unique is its interactive learning style, which helps beginners understand concepts in a practical way.

The course covers topics such as:

- Fundamentals of artificial intelligence  
- Algorithmic thinking  
- Machine learning  
- The impact of AI on society  

After successfully completing the exercises, learners receive a **completely free digital certificate**.

🔗 **Course link:** [Click here to visit the website](https://www.elementsofai.nl)

---

### 3. AI Fundamentals – IBM SkillsBuild

![Coursera Academy](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/imsfaouan/images-kh/refs/heads/main/skillsbuild%20academy.jpg)

The company **IBM** offers an excellent course titled **AI Fundamentals** through its educational platform **IBM SkillsBuild**.

This course aims to introduce beginners to the key concepts of artificial intelligence in a clear and simple way.

The course includes several essential topics such as:

- Introduction to artificial intelligence  
- Basics of machine learning  
- Using AI in business  
- Ethics of artificial intelligence  

After completing the course, learners receive **Digital Badges** recognized by IBM, which can be added to your professional profile or LinkedIn account.

🔗 **Course link:** [Click here to visit the website](https://skillsbuild.org/students/course-catalog/artificial-intelligence)

## Practical Example: How to start your AI learning journey

If you are a complete beginner, you can follow these steps:

1. Start with **AI for Everyone** to understand the big picture of artificial intelligence.  
2. Then move to **Elements of AI** to gain deeper knowledge of the core concepts.  
3. Finally, take **IBM AI Fundamentals** to explore practical applications and ethics.

This approach will give you a solid foundation in the field without needing to learn programming at the beginning.
